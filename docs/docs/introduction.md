## Personas

For this project, we have identified two main personas: the retailer and the customer. These personas represent the key stakeholders in the retail industry and are essential to the success of the Retail3 platform.

### Customer

A customer is an individual or organization that purchases goods or services from a retailer. Customers are the lifeblood of any retail business, as they provide the revenue needed to sustain operations and grow the business. Customers have a wide range of needs, preferences, and expectations when it comes to shopping, and retailers must work hard to meet and exceed these expectations to attract and retain customers.

### Retailer
A retailer is a business or individual that sells goods or services directly to consumers for personal or household use. They typically operate from physical stores, online platforms, or both, and may purchase products from wholesalers or manufacturers to resell to end customers at a markup. Retailers play a crucial role in the distribution chain by providing access to products and services for consumers.

## Features ðŸ”¥

- **Wallet**: Retail3 provides a digital wallet that allows customers to store, manage, and spend their rewards points, discounts, and cashback earnings. By using the Lumx API, customers can access their wallet from any device and use it to pay for purchases at participating retailers.
- **Pool**: Retail3 offers a rewards pool that allows customers to earn additional rewards by shopping at participating retailers. By using the Lumx API, customers can contribute to the rewards pool and earn a share of the rewards based on their contributions and shopping behavior.
- **Marketplace**: Retail3 provides a marketplace that allows customers to discover new products, compare prices, and shop from a wide range of retailers. By using the Lumx API, customers can access personalized recommendations, exclusive deals, and cashback rewards that enhance their shopping experience.
- - **Insights**: Retail3 provides valuable insights into customer behavior, preferences, and trends that help retailers make informed decisions and optimize their operations. By analyzing data from the Lumx API, retailers can identify opportunities for growth, improve customer satisfaction, and stay ahead of the competition.
- **Rewards**: Retail3 offers a rewards program that incentivizes customers to shop smart and earn cashback on their purchases. By using the Lumx API, customers can earn points, discounts, and other rewards that encourage them to shop at participating retailers and share their experiences with others.
- **Security**: Retail3 uses blockchain technology to secure transactions, protect customer data, and ensure trust and transparency in the retail ecosystem. By leveraging the power of the Ethereum blockchain, Retail3 provides a secure, decentralized platform that gives customers and retailers peace of mind when shopping online.

## Technologies

- **Lumx API**: The Lumx API is a powerful tool that allows developers to create innovative applications that leverage the power of the Lumx platform. By integrating the Lumx API into Retail3, we are able to provide a seamless experience for customers and store owners alike.
- **React**: React is a popular JavaScript library for building user interfaces. By using React, we are able to create dynamic, interactive components that enhance the user experience.
- **Node.js**: Node.js is a JavaScript runtime that allows developers to build scalable, high-performance applications. By using Node.js, we are able to create a fast, responsive platform that can handle a large number of users.
- **Solidity**: Solidity is a programming language used to write smart contracts on the Ethereum blockchain. By using Solidity, we are able to create secure, decentralized applications that provide transparency and trust for all users.
